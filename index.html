Publication

Video based Forest Fire And Smoke Detection Using CNN And YoLo

Every year, thousands of forest fires across the world causes disasters and economical loss. There are huge number of well-studied solutions available which can be used to solve this problem. Previously researchers were using sensors to detect fire. But if forest has large area, then it would be difficult to detect fire at early stage. In this paper, we proposed a new approach for forest fire detection, where modern technologies are used. In particular we have used the deep learning methods for recognition and detection of fire and smoke, based on the videos input from the cameras. Deep learning method “convolution neural network” and YoLo can be used for finding the amount of fire and amount of smoke.
